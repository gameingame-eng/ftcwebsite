What is FTC DECODE?

The following is a summary of FIRST Tech Challenge DECODE, the 2025-2026 season game.

Auto (30 seconds)

Robots autonomously score points and move around with no driver input.

TeleOp (2 minutes)

TeleOp is when drivers control their robots to score points, move, and empty the classifier.

Endgame

Endgame is the last 20 seconds of Teleop when robots reace to their parking spots to get a full or partial park.
