Tigger: The 2025 Competition Robot

Our robot is made for offensive play and rapid scoring, beating everyone in the ring. With quick play and fast movement

Our current robot, Tiggerbot.

Key Specifications

Drivetrain

4-Wheel Mecanum

Control/Programming

OnBotJava / FTC SDK

Weight

16.5 lbs (Max 19)

Intake

Built by our amazing builders

The Omnidirectional Drivetrain

We utilize a 4-wheel mecanum drive system, allowing us to speed across the field with no delay whatsoever and flipping into shooting position quickly.

  • Wheels: Mecanum(Omnidirectional)

High-Reach Linear Slide

Our primary scoring mechanism is launching balls out of a tube like opening, also used to load balls at the beginning of the match.

  • Actuation: 1x 5202 GoBILDA Motor
  • Extension: 30 inches of vertical travel
  • Precision: PID control for accurate height stopping

Control System & Software

The robot runs on a Rev Control Hub and is programmed entirely in Java using the FTC SDK. We heavily implement custom classes for drive control and state machines for reliable autonomous routines.

  • Hardware: Rev Control Hub, 2x Servos, 6x Motors
  • Programming: Fixed Auto
  • Source Code: Available on GitHub
